HOW TO PARTICIPATE?

Step 1

Register for the contest by filling the form

Step 2

Create a college review video of up to 5 minutes (as many as you want)
Put on your director's hat & pick one or more topics from the options below

  • How is campus life? Showcase the best bits about your day in college!
  • Two things you wish you could change in your campus
  • The most popular clubs/societies in your college? Which is your favourite and why?
  • What should freshers expect when they become a part of your college?
  • Show us your favourite hangout spot, and tell us why you like it
  • Most interesting things about the course you are studying

Step 3

Mail it to marketing@shiksha.com
If sharing a drive link, please accept the access request from Shiksha gmail ID.

video will be judged on the following criteria
  • Creativity
  • Quality of Story telling & Articulation
  • Authenticity of Information
  • Quality of video packaging: footage, voice, editing

Contest Guidelines

  • Contest Duration: 10th January to 29th February 2024
  • Participants must be currently enrolled students of the college
  • Video length should not exceed more then 5 minutes
  • The video or content should not infringe any third party intellectual property or copyrights
  • It should be original unpublished content
  • Inappropriate or offensive content will be disqualified
  • The video should do complete justice to the topic that has been selected by the creator
  • The video should be mailed to marketing@shiksha.com
    File name format to follow: name_college_course
  • Don't forget to share your contact details on mail body and share access to video drive links on Shiksha Gmail ID

Video Guidelines

  • Videos must be recorded in HD video setting
  • The lighting and audio quality of the video should be good
  • Ensure the video has maximum college coverage
  • Please include original and substantial campus footage
  • Ensure the video is stable and smooth
  • Please DO NOT use any background music
  • The video produced must be in English, Hinglish or Hindi
  • Videos must be recorded in participant’s college campus or areas being talked about in the video
  • Videos should have HD quality footages. To make it vibrant you may include both outdoor or indoor HD quality footages
  • Duration of video should not exceed 5 minutes
